A Look at Some of Naanghirisa Activities

The relevance of Naanghirisa towards the development of our community is largely seen in its key activities; The PASS Project, Butaleja Going Green, NSACCO, The Scholarship Scheme among others.

The PASS Project

Naanghirisa chose education as one of its priority areas of focus and developed the PASS project (Promoting Academic Success in Schools). The goal of this project is to contribute to the improvement of education standards in Butaleja in general.

Learn More

Scholarship Initiative

The Dr. Margeret Munghereera Scolarship for Girl Child aims to support the education of bright but disadvantaged girls from who are otherwise, unable to progress with studies

Learn More

Butaleja Going Green

Going Green handles enviroment protection through advocacy for Tree Planting. Going Green is implemented in schools through the appointed schools ambassadors. The idea is to make Butaleja green.

Networking and Fundraising

Networking aims at bringing all together stakeholders to know each other, share different opportunities and boosting the afromentioned projects and making sure they operate smoothly.
