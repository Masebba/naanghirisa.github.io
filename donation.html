Naanghirisa is possible thanks to the kind people who support our nonprofit each month. If you want to be part in helping disadvantaged girls and boys get education, here is how you can support.

Naanghirisa's budget is much smaller than most non-profit organisations. We can admit that we are bad fundraisers because e haven't brought in professional fundraisers. But we can tell you exactly where all the money is going because we oversee everything. We can also tell you that - on a budget of as little as a few hundred dollars per year, we have been able to accomplish a lot for our community.

For as low as the price of less than a cup of coffee, your donations can help support Naanghirisa by:

1. Pay tuition for bright but disadvataged girls in Butaleja

2. Support uphold good performance for Butaleja students in schools

3. Prepare and secure advance future females doctors

As a direct result of this noble effort:

1. 43 lessprivileged girls and boys are already in school as of 2024, in secondary and university levels

2. Improved performance among Butaleja students through the PASS project

3. Hundreds of families are going to be directly impacted by our work and we have a guaranteed ripple effect to our community.

1. Naanghirisa help share the message of the scholarship opportunities through our team and local governement.

2. Naanghirisa help build partnership with various schools for possible tuition waivers

3. Support students with personal and school requirements

4. As well as caryy out new process of those joing post-primary education every year.

No one has ever become poor by giving
